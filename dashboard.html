Welcome to the Oulankajoki Supersite Virtual Tour Dashboard — a dual-panel interactive platform combining a browser-based VR explorer and a live streaming window, developed within the Digital Waters (DIWA) Flagship Program at the University of Oulu, Finland.
🌐
Left Panel — Interactive Web Explorer
Explore the Oulankajoki Supersite directly in your browser. Navigate freely using mouse and keyboard through the georeferenced point cloud and satellite basemap streamed from Cesium ion. Click on markers to open scientific data panels and site photography.
📺
Right Panel — Live VR Stream
Watch a real-time live stream of the Meta Quest 3 VR session as it happens during the EGU 2026 event. The presenter navigates inside the immersive VR environment and the experience is broadcast live — accessible to anyone worldwide through this dashboard.
